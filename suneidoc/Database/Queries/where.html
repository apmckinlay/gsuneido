where (restriction/selection)

query where expression

The result of where is a table with the same columns as the input but with just the rows where the expression is true.

date part quantity
00/09/01 abc 5
00/09/01 def 10
00/09/02 abc 1
00/09/02 ghi 20
where part = "abc" =
date part quantity
00/09/01 abc 5
00/09/02 abc 1
date part quantity
00/09/01 abc 5
00/09/01 def 10
00/09/02 abc 1
00/09/02 ghi 20
where quantity > 10 and
date = #20000902		 =
date part quantity
00/09/02 ghi 20

Note: Query expressions do not support the full language expression syntax. In particular, calling member functions is not supported, only simple global functions. You can usually work around this by defining global helper functions to make any necessary method calls.
